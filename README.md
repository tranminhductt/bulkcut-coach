## 📸 How to Analyze Food Photos

bulkcut-coach uses your food photos to estimate calories. To do this:

1. Take a clear photo of your meal with your phone or camera.  
2. Save the photo on your computer or a USB drive.  
3. Open bulkcut-coach and enter:  
   `bulkcut-coach photo C:\path\to\your\photo.jpg`  
4. Press Enter. The program will show calorie info based on the photo.

The accuracy depends on the photo quality and food visibility.

## 🔢 Calculate BMR and TDEE

Your BMR is the calories your body uses at rest. TDEE adds the calories burned through movement and exercise.

To calculate your BMR:

1. Enter `bulkcut-coach bmr`.  
2. The program will ask for your height, weight, age, and gender.  
3. After you input these, it will display your BMR.

To estimate your TDEE:

1. Enter `bulkcut-coach tdee`.  
2. Provide your activity level when asked.  
3. The TDEE number will appear.

Knowing these helps you plan your diet and workouts.
